Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our car care services in Vijayawada.

What is the difference between 2K paint and regular paint?
2K paint (two-component paint) is a professional-grade automotive paint that uses a hardener mixed with the base coat. This creates a chemical reaction that produces a much harder, more durable finish compared to regular 1K paints. At 2K Car Care Vijayawada, we exclusively use 2K paints because they offer superior UV resistance, chemical resistance, and longevity. The finish lasts 2-3 times longer than conventional paints and maintains its gloss for years.
What is Glasurit paint and why is it premium?
Glasurit is a premium automotive paint brand manufactured in both Germany and France, part of the BASF Coatings group. It's considered one of the world's finest car paint systems, used by luxury car manufacturers like Mercedes-Benz, BMW, and Porsche. Glasurit paints offer exceptional color accuracy, superior durability, excellent weather resistance, and a mirror-like finish. At 2K Car Care Vijayawada, we're proud to use Glasurit premium paints for customers who want the absolute best quality for their vehicles.
What type of paints does 2K Car Care Vijayawada use?
We use two types of premium paints: 2K (two-component) paints for standard professional-grade finishes, and Glasurit premium paints (manufactured in Germany and France) for customers who want the highest quality. Both are professional automotive paint systems that offer superior durability, color accuracy, and longevity compared to regular paints. We choose the paint type based on your vehicle, budget, and desired finish.

How long does ceramic coating take and how long does it last?
Ceramic coating typically takes 2-3 days depending on your car's condition and the level of paint correction needed. The coating itself cures within 24-48 hours. Once applied, professional ceramic coating lasts 3-5 years with proper maintenance. We also offer graphene coating which lasts 5-7 years with even better durability.
Do you offer warranty on your services?
Yes! All our coating services come with a written warranty. Ceramic coatings are covered for 3-5 years, graphene coatings for 5-7 years, and paint jobs have comprehensive coverage. We stand behind our work if something goes wrong due to our application, we'll fix it at no extra cost.
What is the difference between Nano, Ceramic, and Graphene coating?
Nano coating uses nano-particles to create a thin protective layer that lasts 6-12 months it's a good entry-level option. Ceramic coating uses silicon dioxide for much harder, longer-lasting protection (3-5 years) with superior hydrophobic properties. Graphene coating is the newest technology it offers even better hardness, heat resistance, and a deeper mirror gloss than ceramic, lasting 5-7 years. Our team can help you choose the right option based on your budget and needs.
