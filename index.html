Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our car care services in Vijayawada.

What is the difference between 2K paint and regular paint?
2K paint (two-component paint) is a professional-grade automotive paint that uses a hardener mixed with the base coat. This creates a chemical reaction that produces a much harder, more durable finish. The finish lasts 2-3 times longer than conventional paints.
What is Glasurit paint and why is it premium?
Glasurit is a premium automotive paint brand manufactured in both Germany and France, part of the BASF Coatings group. Used by Mercedes-Benz, BMW, and Porsche. Offers exceptional color accuracy, superior durability, and a mirror-like finish.
What car wash maintenance packages do you offer?

Which districts of Andhra Pradesh do you serve?
We proudly serve customers from all 26 districts of Andhra Pradesh including Krishna, Guntur, Visakhapatnam, East/West Godavari, Srikakulam, Kurnool, Kadapa, Anantapur, Chittoor, and newer districts like Bapatla, NTR, Palnadu, Eluru.
How long does ceramic coating last?
Professional ceramic coating lasts 3-5 years with proper maintenance. We also offer graphene coating which lasts 5-7 years with even better durability.
Do you offer warranty on your services?
Yes! All coating services come with written warranty. Ceramic: 3-5 years, Graphene: 5-7 years, Paint jobs: comprehensive coverage.
