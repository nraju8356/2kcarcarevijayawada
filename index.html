Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services.

What types of cars do you service?
We service all types of vehicles — hatchbacks, sedans, SUVs, and luxury cars. No matter the make or model, we deliver factory-quality results.
How long does ceramic coating take?
Ceramic coating typically takes 2-3 days depending on your car's condition and the level of paint correction needed. The coating itself cures within 24-48 hours.
Do you offer warranty on your services?
Yes! All our coating services come with a warranty. Ceramic coatings are covered for 3-5 years, graphene coatings for 5-7 years, and paint protection films for 7-10 years.
What is the difference between Nano and Ceramic coating?
Nano coating uses nano-particles to create a thin protective layer (lasts 6-12 months), while ceramic coating uses silicon dioxide for much harder, longer-lasting protection (3-5 years) with superior hydrophobic properties.
