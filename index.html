Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services at 2K Car Care Vijayawada.

What services does 2K Car Care offer?
We offer a wide range of car care services including denting, painting, nano coat, ceramic coat, graphene coat, rubbing & polish, teflon coating, interior detailing, underbody anti-rust coat, glass cooling film, basic accessories, water wash, dechrome, and colour customization.
How long does ceramic coating last?
A professional ceramic coating applied at 2K Car Care typically lasts 3–5 years depending on the grade of coating used and how well the car is maintained. We recommend periodic ceramic maintenance washes to extend its lifespan and keep the gloss intact.
What is the difference between nano coat, ceramic coat, and graphene coat?
Nano coat is an entry-level protective layer offering basic water and UV repellency. Ceramic coat is a mid-to-high level protection with excellent hardness, gloss, and durability. Graphene coat is the latest premium technology — it offers superior hardness, better heat resistance, and a deeper mirror-like gloss compared to ceramic.
How much does a full body paint job cost?
Pricing depends on the car's size, type of paint, and extent of work required. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden charges. Please call us on +91 85199 57776 or visit our center in Vijayawada for a free inspection and quote.
How long does a full detailing service take?
A basic wash takes 1–2 hours. Full detailing with interior cleaning takes 3–5 hours. Ceramic or graphene coating requires 1–2 days including curing time. Full body painting or dismantling jobs may take 3–7 days depending on the complexity.
Is it safe to get underbody anti-rust coating done?
Absolutely. Underbody anti-rust coating is highly recommended, especially during and after monsoon season. It protects your car's chassis, frame, and metal components from moisture, salt, and road debris that cause rust and corrosion over time.
What is QRS – Quick Repair Service?
QRS (Quick Repair Service) is our fast-turnaround solution for minor scratches, small dents, and paint chips. Instead of a full repaint, we fix only the affected area — saving you time and cost. Most QRS jobs are completed within a few hours.

Do you work on all types of cars?
Yes! We service all types of vehicles — hatchbacks, sedans, SUVs, MUVs, and luxury cars. Our team is trained to handle every car type with the right products and techniques suited to each vehicle.
